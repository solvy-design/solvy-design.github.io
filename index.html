Peak

Measuring progress in physiotherapy

Peak is a physiotherapy tool to measure progress in rehabilitation processes.

Peak is a supplement to finding and evaluating the client’s problem and makes it possible to continually follow the treatment effect.

If the client is following their own progress, it is scientifically proven to contribute to the motivation of the client, which optimizes the rehabilitation process. 

Product system

Peak is a product system consisting of a measuring unit with two mounting solutions for a stable mounting securing accurate measurements.

You can choose to between the two mounting solutions; a strap or disposable stickers both for fast and easy cleaning between clients.

The measuring results are displayed in the Peak application, which the measuring unit sends the results to. 

Features

 

Accurate measurements

Peak is verified in scientific research to provide objective documentation from accurate digital measurements.

Measurements

Peak measures joint range of motion and the quality of movement by velocity, acceleration and jerkiness. Furthermore, Peak can measure the off-set of joint repositioning.

Attachment

Peak is fast and easy to apply and use.

Cleaning

Peak is fast and easy to clean between clients.

Medical Device

Peak is verified as a medical device.

Peak application

Peak is controlled through the Peak application. Here you can measure the client’s progress or current state with joint range of motion – with this feature, the quality of movement will also be measured during the measurement movement, which indicates if the client is experiencing pain in a certain position.

The feature of joint repositioning can also be accessed to test the client’s proprioceptive sense to support the identification of the disturbances in the postural control system of the client.

All the measured data can be stored to the data library for easy access. 

One product many placements

Peak can measure joint range of motion and the quality of movement of all extremity joints + the neck and back. The product system is developed to be applied for the standard measurement movements for joint range of motion.

Where existing products for measuring joint mobility are influenced by human errors during use, Peak eliminates these errors of human interference, when applied following the guidelines.
