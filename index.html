Moti is a physiotherapy tool to measure progress in rehabilitation processes. 

Moti is a supplement to find and evaluate the client’s problem and makes it possible to continually follow the treatment effect. 

If the client is following their own progress, it is scientifically proven to contribute to the motivation of the client, which optimizes the rehabilitation process.

Product system

Moti is a product system consisting of a measuring unit with two mounting solutions for a stable mounting securing accurate measurements.

You can choose to between the two mounting solutions; a strap or disposable stickers both for fast and easy cleaning between clients.

The measuring results are displayed in the Moti application, which the measuring unit sends the results to.

 

Features

Accurate measurements

Moti is verified in scientific research to provide objective documentation from accurate digital measurements.

Measurements

Moti measures joint range of motion and the quality of movement by velocity, acceleration and jerkiness. Furthermore, Moti can measure the off-set of joint repositioning.

Attachment

Moti is fast and easy to apply and use.

Cleaning

Moti is fast and easy to clean between clients.

Medical Device

Moti is verified as a medical device.
